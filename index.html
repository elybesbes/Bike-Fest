Program

9H 30

Check-In

The process of check-in involves verifying participants' registration and credentials to ensure their eligibility for the competition.

9H 30

Zumba activity

Zumba is a high-energy fitness dance class that combines Latin and international music for a fun and effective workout.

10H 30

Start of the Duathlon

A duathlon is a multisport event that involves running, biking, and running again, typically in a run-bike-run sequence.

11H

Coffee Break

A coffee break is a short, refreshing pause during which participants take a break from their activities to enjoy a cup of coffee and relax.

11H 30

Treasure Hunt

A treasure hunt is a recreational activity in which participants follow a series of hints to find a hidden treasure.

12H 30

Prize Distribution

The first-place winner receives a 6-month gym membership, while the second-place winner gets a 3-month membership.

Organizers

Sup'Bike is an active club within SUP'COM, with 5 active members on its executive board and 40 members on the club. The club's mission is to organize events and activities that promote sports participation and a healthy lifestyle among SUP'COM and Technoparks students and workers.
